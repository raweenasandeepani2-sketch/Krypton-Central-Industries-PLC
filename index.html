Technical Compliance

Professional installation standards for Krypton electrical systems.

N Series Gang Switches (SLS 1000)

  • Must be installed in a standard 35mm deep mounting box.
  • Ensure terminal screws are firm but not over-torqued to avoid internal stress.

13A Switched Sockets (SLS 734)

  • Earthing is mandatory for all SLS 734 socket installations.
  • Maximum loop-in wiring: 2.5mm² twin and earth cable.
